Multiroom Config

A multiroom audio configuration consists of a Sender and one or more Receivers. The Sender should be a dedicated moOde system configured to output to the integrated Headphone jack or HDMI port but not connected to speakers or headphones. It functions solely as a Music Server that Sends its audio output to one or more multiroom Receivers.
